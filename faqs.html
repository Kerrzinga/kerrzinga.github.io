FAQ

Common questions

Everything you might want to know before getting started.

Is there any upfront cost?
No. You don't pay a thing until you've seen your first draft and decided you love it. We take all the risk.
When do payments start?
Only once you're happy with the draft and give us the go-ahead to launch. If you're not happy, you walk away owing nothing — no awkward conversations, no invoices.
Is there a minimum contract?
No lock-in, no minimum term. Stay as long as you want and cancel anytime. We'd rather earn your business every month.
What's included in the $40/month?
Everything — your domain name, hosting, mobile-friendly design, Google setup, a contact form, and ongoing updates whenever you need them. No hidden extras.
How many pages can I have?
As many as your business needs. Most of our sites have 3–5 pages (Home, About, Services, Contact), but we'll build whatever makes sense for you.
How long does it take?
You'll have your free first draft within a week of filling in the form. Once you approve it, the site is usually live within two weeks — often faster.
Can I make changes after the site is live?
Absolutely — that's part of the plan. Need to update your prices, swap a photo, add a new testimonial? Just send us a message and we'll handle it. No limits, no extra charge.
What if I already have a website?
No problem. We can build you something new and switch over when you're ready, or take a look at what you've got and suggest improvements.
Do I own the website?
You own your domain name and all your content. If you ever decide to leave, we'll help you take what's yours.
