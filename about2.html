About Kerrzinga

Simple, affordable web design for Whangaparaoa businesses.

Kerrzinga is a web design service built around one idea: small businesses deserve a great website without the big upfront bill that usually comes with one.

Based in Whangaparaoa and working with businesses across the Hibiscus Coast and beyond, Kerrzinga delivers professional websites that are found on Google, work perfectly on every device, and stay current over time — all for one simple monthly fee, no setup cost, no lock-in contract.

There had to be a better way

Too many small business owners were ending up in the same situation. They had ideas, they wanted to take their business seriously online, and they knew they needed a website — but the whole process felt more expensive, slower, and more awkward than it should have been.

Some were put off by big upfront quotes. Others paid the money, waited months, and still ended up with something that didn't quite feel right. Then when they wanted a small change later, the meter was running again.

The tools for building websites have moved on massively. It's now possible to deliver something professional and high quality far more efficiently than before. But many small businesses were still being offered the same old model: large upfront cost, then more charges later.

Kerrzinga is a simpler alternative.

How it works

Think of it like a phone contract. You don't walk into a phone shop and pay the full cost of a handset upfront. You spread the cost over time and get both the phone and the service that keeps it useful, wrapped into one predictable monthly payment.

Kerrzinga works the same way. From day one you get a professionally designed website that works properly across phones, tablets, and computers. Your monthly payment covers everything that keeps it running — domain, hosting, updates, and ongoing support. No large upfront bill. No hidden extras. No nasty surprises.

And because the first draft is built completely free, you see the finished design before you spend a cent. If it's not right, you walk away owing nothing.
