The story of Kerrzinga

I started Kerrzinga because I felt there had to be a better way for small businesses to get a decent website.

I had seen too many business owners end up in the same situation. They had ideas, they wanted to take their business seriously, and they knew they needed a website, but the whole thing often felt more expensive, slower, and more awkward than it should have been. Some were put off by big upfront quotes. Others paid the money, waited ages, and still ended up with something that did not quite feel right. Then if they wanted a few changes later, they were back to paying extra again.

That never made much sense to me.

The tools for building websites have improved massively over the last few years. It is now possible to create professional, high-quality websites more efficiently than ever before. But a lot of small businesses were still being offered the same old model: big upfront cost, then more charges later.

I thought there was room to do it differently.

That is why I started Kerrzinga. I wanted to offer something simpler, more affordable, and easier to live with. Something that gives small businesses a professional website without needing a big lump sum at the start.

Think of it like a phone contract. You do not walk into a phone shop and pay the full cost of a handset upfront. You spread the cost over time, and in return you get both the phone and the service that keeps it useful, all wrapped into one predictable monthly payment. Kerrzinga works in much the same way.

From day one, you get a professionally designed website that works properly across phones, tablets, and computers. Then your monthly payment covers the things that keep it running: your domain name, hosting, routine updates, and ongoing support. No large upfront bill. No hidden extras. No nasty surprises.

That approach just feels more sensible to me.

Kerrzinga is my way of making professional websites more accessible for small businesses. Clear pricing, less friction, and a service that feels like it is actually built around the customer.

You see the finished design before you spend a cent. If it's not right, you walk away owing nothing. - Dave Kerr, Founder
